About us

The company was founded by Micheal Pete and Michelle Pete in 1999 after Purdue's Women's Basketball team won the NCAA championships. Michelle, a player on the Purdue's Womens Basketball team, married her boyfriend Micheal who was a basketball fan and decided they want to continue their life with basketball in their lives. Now Micheal and Michelle's March Madness sells all kinds of products relates to the sports of basketball, and is determined to bring the basketball fans here at Purdue their best experiences.
